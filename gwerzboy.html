~ Gwerzboy ♫ ⬉



"Gwerz Boy" is the name of the "chip-trad" album I released in May 2023 (a "Gwerz" is a type of Breton song). "Gwerzboy" is the name of the gameboy videogame I entirely developped to go with the release of my album.




The songs of my album were composed on two trackers, LSDJ and Dirtywave M8. The idea was to replace the CD with a cartridge, on which you could listen to the songs generated in real-time by the gameboy sound processor. A minigame was added to illustrate one of the songs, "Brestois plutôt que Français". The game was built with GBStudio and is available both as a Gameboy cartridge and an in-browser game : try it below (english version) or on itch.io (french and english versions) !

Enjoy the 6 tracks of the album in their chiptune arrangement, with illustrations by Maître Selecto in 160x144px and 4 shades of green !
Visit Brest to help Georges, bothered by the cops !



I also made a video illustrating one of the song, using a custom version of the game and real-time corruption techniques on the ROM.
The result is a glitchy pixel-art Breton karaoke ⚘
