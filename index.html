Antoine Le Dreff
René Danger

Antoine Le Dreff (b. 1993) is a sound artist based in Le Mans (France), with training in architecture (ENSA Paris-Malaquais) and sound design (TALM Le Mans – IRCAM).
He is interested in obsolete technologies, instrument making, and sound in space. As a musician and producer, he is mostly active as René Danger.
He co-founded the label Editentités, a platform for exploring traditional and futuristic sounds, and is a member of Ondorphine, a collective of sound artists and radio hacktivists.
