Antoine Le Dreff
~ René Danger

Antoine Le Dreff (b. 1993) is a sound artist based in Rennes (France), with training in architecture (ENSA Paris-Malaquais) and sound design (TALM Le Mans – IRCAM).
He is interested in electronic and acoustic instrument making, radio waves, obsolete technologies, and sound in space. As a musician and producer, he is mostly active as René Danger.
In his performances, René Danger merges his voice with modular synthesizers and his gameboys, mixing traditional melodies and electronic soundscapes : let's call it gwerz-dub or chip-trad.
He co-founded the label Editentités, a platform for exploring traditional and futuristic sounds, and is a member of Ondorphine, a collective of sound artists and radio hacktivists.
